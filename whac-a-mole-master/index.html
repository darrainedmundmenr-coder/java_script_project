Whack-a-Mole

Score: 0

Time: 60

How to Play & Mechanics

🎮 Core Objective: Achieve the highest score possible by reacting quickly, hitting moles accurately, and managing your 3 lives.
🐹 Normal Mole: Appears frequently. Gives +1 point when clicked. Failing to hit it before it disappears costs you 1 life.
🟡 Golden Mole: Appears rarely and disappears faster. Hit it for +3 bonus points. No penalty if missed.
⚡ Modes & Scaling:
  • Classic Mode: Slower mole spawn rates for casual play.
  • Challenge Mode: Faster spawns and shorter windows to test your reflexes.
🛑 Game Over: The game ends if the 60-second timer runs out or if you lose all your hearts.
